## 4.1 System overview and architecture

### Overview

**SpendWiseApp** is a personal expense management web application for tracking transactions, budgets, and savings goals.

The system is designed on AWS with a cloud-native approach to improve:
- Scalability under changing workloads.
- Reliability compared with single-server setups.
- Operational visibility and maintainability.
